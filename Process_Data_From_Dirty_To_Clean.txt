==================================== MODUL 1 =======================================

Data integrity is the accuracy, completeness, consistency, and trustworthiness of data throughout its lifecycle.

Types of insufficient data:
1. Data only from one source
2. Data that keeps updating
3. Data outdated
4. Geographical-data based

How to solve this:
1. identify trends with the available data
2. wait to more data if waktu cukup
3. talk with stakeholder and adjust objective
4. Look for new dataset

Menentukan sample size
1. Tidak boleh kurang dari 30 data
2. confidence level yang digunakan biasanya 95%

Statistical power adalah peluang terdapatnya meaningful result saat test
Minimum statistical power agar dikatakan statistical significant adalah 0,8 

Jika suatu test atau study dikatakan statistical significant, maka hasilnya real dan tidak error karna disebabkan oleh sesuatu yang random
